Making a Lot of Space for Conversation

One of the best experiences from urban camping through couch surfing was the chance to hear so many different stories about how the world works.

I like to compare notes in terms of perspective, but I'm also just becoming addicted to the genre of conversational stories.

Crafted stories through literature, movies, etc. have a beginning, middle and end. But conversational stories are interactive, organic and surprising.

Crafted stories are designed to generate an effect on the reader (and I love them too) but conversational stories are candid interpretations, fragmented explanations, unique riddles, things you've never heard before. When people choose to be open, their stories are not formulaic. The stories are not craft. And I wish I could hear them all.
