Spliced Footage
oil paint, 3 cotton canvases
.60 x 1.20 meter
2000
middle canvas collection Roel Vis and Tineke Speksnijder

The images for this painting came from a short animation.

At the time, I was experimenting with making a work as both an animation and as a painting.

Perhaps playing with the composition in two different ways/media at the same time would influence the outcome of both pieces.

An animation also goes with the painting,
"Madonna with or without Child."
